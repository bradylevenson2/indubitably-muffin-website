Refund Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

1. Digital Product Purchases

One-time digital product purchases (apps, tools, content) are refundable within 14 days of purchase if the product has a technical issue that prevents normal use.

2. Subscription Services

Monthly and annual subscription fees are refundable within 7 days of initial subscription. Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period with no further charges.

3. Refund Process

To request a refund, contact us at connect@indubitablymuffin.com with your order details. Refunds are processed within 5-10 business days.

4. Non-Refundable Items

In-app purchases, digital content downloads, and consumable items are generally non-refundable once delivered and accessed by the user.

5. Contact Us

For refund requests or questions, email connect@indubitablymuffin.com
